CHICKEN WINGS

Ingredients

  • Chicken wings
  • Salt and pepper
  • Honey
  • Soy sauce
  • Vegetable or canola oil
  • Ketchup
  • Garlic cloves
  • Cayenne pepper

Description

Chicken wings, a beloved finger-food favorite, feature tender, juicy chicken pieces seasoned to perfection, deep-fried to crispy golden perfection, and often coated in a flavorful sauce for a mouthwatering and satisfying snack or appetizer.

Recipe credit:

  • RECEPIE GIRL

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (176°C).
  2. Spray a 13x9-inch or larger casserole dish with nonstick spray.
  3. Cut the chicken wings into 3 sections; discard the wing tip section. Coat with salt and pepper.
  4. Combine the remaining ingredients in a medium bowl until well mixed.
  5. Place the wings in a shallow casserole, pour the sauce over them, and bake until the chicken is well done and the sauce is caramelized, about 1 hour.

Notes

If you are preparing this recipe as GLUTEN FREE, just be sure to use brands of soy sauce and ketchup that are known to be GF.
